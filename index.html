FAQs

What is SpaceNXT?

SpaceNXT is an out-of-this-world co-creation space for visionaries, techpreneurs and innovators aimed at encouraging open innovation internally and in collaboration with our external community.

Where is SpaceNXT located?

SpaceNXT is located on the 2nd floor, Union Bank Head Office, Stallion Plaza, 36 Marina Road, Marina, Lagos.
Locate on Maps

How can I book a space in SpaceNXT?

Please select the “Book Now” tab at the top right corner of the homepage.

How much does it cost to book a co-working space?

Our rates are ₦3,000 daily, ₦12,000 weekly (five days) and ₦45,000 monthly. In addition, returning customers enjoy a 5% discount on new bookings.

Can my booking confirmation be used by another person?

No, a booking confirmation can only be used by the individual that registered and cannot be used by a third party.

Can I work overnight at SpaceNXT?

No. Our co-working spaces are currently only available from 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m., Monday through Friday.

What happens if I misplace my voucher?

Once your booking is confirmed, SpaceNXT generates and sends e-vouchers to the registered email address If you do not receive your e-voucher, please send an email to mail with your full name and payment receipt.
