FAQs

What is SpaceNXT?

SpaceNXT is Union bank's tech and innovation hub that was built to promote innovation and encourage collaboration within the tech ecosystem in Nigeria. SpaceNXT also acts as a co-creation workspace for innovators, creators and techpreneurs.

Where is SpaceNXT located?

SpaceNXT is located at Union bank's Head office on 36, Marina Road, Lagos.
Locate on Maps

How can I book a space in SpaceNXT?

Please click “Book Now” on our homepage.

What are the price rates for booking co-working spaces?

Our rates are 3000 daily, 12,000 weekly/5 days and 45,000 monthly. In addition, members get a 5% discount on other spaces.

Can my booking confirmation be used by another person?

No. A booking confirmation can only be used by the individual that registered and cannot be used by a third party.

Can I work overnight at SpaceNXT?

Currently, our co-working space is only available between 8am -6pm from Mondays to Fridays

What happens if I misplace my voucher?

SpaceNXT utilizes e-vouchers that are generated and sent to the registered email address once payment is confirmed. You can always refer to your mail for your e-voucher. In an event you are unable to still locate your e-voucher, please send us a mail containing your payment receipt and full name.
